About Me

I am currently an Algorithm Engineer at Alibaba. Previously, I received my MSc degree from Sun Yat-sen University in 2023, under supvervision of Prof. Ying Shen. My main research interests are Deep Learning and Natural Language Processing, with particular focus on Federated Learning, Text Generation and Model Compression.
